My Journey

Every path tells a story

Four defining chapters shaped who I am today — from a curious student in a small town to building AI products used by thousands.

Chapter One

The Spark of Curiosity

Growing up in a small town in Anhui province, I spent afternoons disassembling radios and writing simple programs on a second-hand computer. A high school teacher handed me a book on machine learning that changed everything — suddenly the world felt like a puzzle waiting to be solved.

Key takeaway: Curiosity is the most powerful engine for growth.

Chapter Two

Deep in the Lab

At Tsinghua University, I joined a natural language processing research lab. Late nights debugging models, weekend paper readings, and the thrill of a first publication in ACL taught me the discipline of rigorous thinking. I learned that breakthroughs come from persistence, not inspiration alone.

Key takeaway: Discipline and rigor turn ideas into real results.

Chapter Three

The Leap to Industry

Joining a top AI company in Beijing as a founding engineer was terrifying and exhilarating. We shipped a conversational AI product in 6 months that reached 500K users. I discovered that impact comes from shipping, not from perfecting — and that leading a small team requires empathy as much as expertise.

Key takeaway: Ship fast, listen deeply, iterate always.

Chapter Four

Building My Own Thing

In 2024, I co-founded a company focused on AI-powered educational tools for underserved communities. Today we serve 120+ schools across rural China. The mission is simple: make great education accessible to every child, regardless of geography or income.

Key takeaway: Technology is only as valuable as the lives it improves.
