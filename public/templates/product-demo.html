Everything you need to know before getting started.

What frameworks are supported?
PixelPack Pro components ship as clean HTML/CSS and also include React, Vue, and Svelte variants. You can use them with any CSS framework including Tailwind, and they work great with Next.js, Nuxt, and SvelteKit.
Can I use this for client projects?
Absolutely! The Pro license allows unlimited personal and client projects. The Team Bundle extends this to your entire team (up to 10 seats). You can build and sell products using our components.
Do I get free updates?
Yes. All purchases include one full year of free updates, including new components, templates, and improvements. After the first year, you can renew at a 50% discount or keep using what you have forever.
What if I'm not satisfied?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. If PixelPack Pro doesn't work for you, email us and we'll process a full refund within 48 hours.
