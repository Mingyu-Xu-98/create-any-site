FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How does the free trial work?
You get 14 days of full Pro features with no credit card required. At the end of the trial, you can downgrade to Free or upgrade to keep your Pro features.
Can I switch plans at any time?
Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time. If you downgrade mid-cycle, you'll keep Pro features until the end of your billing period.
Is my data encrypted?
Yes. All data is encrypted at rest with AES-256 and in transit with TLS 1.3. Enterprise customers can also bring their own encryption keys.
What integrations do you support?
We support 200+ integrations including PostgreSQL, MySQL, MongoDB, Snowflake, BigQuery, Salesforce, Stripe, Slack, and more. Check our integrations page for the full list.
Do you offer on-premise deployment?
Yes, on-premise and private cloud deployments are available on our Enterprise plan. Our team will work with you to set up a deployment that meets your compliance requirements.
