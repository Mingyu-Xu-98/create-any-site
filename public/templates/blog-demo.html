About Me

Hi, I'm Siyuan. I'm a designer and developer based in Shanghai. By day I craft interfaces at a design studio; by night I write about the things I'm learning and the places I go.

This blog is my thinking-out-loud space. I believe good design is invisible, good code is readable, and a good life is built on curiosity. I write in both English and Chinese, often mixing the two when one language captures a thought better than the other.

When I'm not at a screen, you'll find me in a bookstore, on a hiking trail, or attempting (badly) to sketch in a Moleskine.
